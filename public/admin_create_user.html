System Role & Permissions

Select the appropriate role for this user. Each role has different permissions and access levels.

Evidence Officer
Can upload evidence, view assigned cases, and manage evidence in custody.
Forensic Analyst
Can analyze evidence, verify integrity, and submit analysis reports.
Administrator
Full system access. Can manage users, settings, and all system functions.
Court Officer
Can view evidence for court proceedings and generate presentation materials.
Investigator
Can access evidence for investigation purposes and request analysis.
Legal Officer
Can review evidence for legal proceedings and prepare case files.
Role permissions are automatically assigned. Users will only see features relevant to their role.
